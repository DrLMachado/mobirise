AeroPlann is a flight planning school focused on the solutions for learning and professional improvement of civil aviators who have the interest in flying international routes.

OUR BUSINESS

Aeroplann offers high quality technical products in simple and accessible language.
We have developed a portfolio of products that enables the full integration of aerospace specialist engineering with civil aviation. This combination provides a unique package mixing the conceptual and practical aspects of our courses, directly serving the needs of our customers, providing products of unique qualities to the market.

OUR HISTORY

AeroPlann started when two friends decided to merge their complementary knowledge on aviation and engineering into an online flight planning school.
